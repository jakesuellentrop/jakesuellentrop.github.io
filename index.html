Promo Depot is the largest site I currently manage. It pulls its product catalog from a variety of proprietary vendor feeds, and provides product customization during shopping.

Underneath the main umbrella store, there are also privately branded "mini stores" for individual brands. All customer, order, shipping, and packaging information is accessed through a common Admin interface that drives each business unit's processes.
